### About Me

I'm a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the **University of Colorado Colorado Springs (UCCS)**, researching the
intersection of **Quantum Computing**, **Machine Learning**, and **AI for Software Engineering**. 


- 🔐 Contributor to the **NSF-sponsored TianoShield** project 
- ⚛️ Contributor to the **Colorado OEDIT-sponsored Q-Dev** project
- 🌱 Co-mentoring undergraduate researchers through the **NSF REU** program

- 📫 Reach me at **nsiavash@uccs.edu**
